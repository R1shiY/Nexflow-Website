The Problem

The current primary method for measuring Insulin Resistance Levels - HOMA-IR - only occurs at a single point of time, with the patient undergoing strenuous fasting and measurements for the level. This measurement isn't taken frequently, even though the output (Insulin Resistance Level) is the single most early predictor of Type 2 Diabetes.

Our Solution

NexFlow offers real-time insulin resistance monitoring through non-invasive methods, making early intervention accessible and affordable.

IRScore

A real-time, AI-generated score from 0 to 100 that quantifies your risk of insulin resistance. Simply input lifestyle and biomarker data and receive an easy-to-understand score that updates as new data is logged.

Trajectory Panel

Visualize how your IRScore evolves over time, helping you see the effects of specific habits. Track daily behaviors and biomarker updates to identify patterns, measure progress, and course-correct in real-time.

Recommendation System

Receive personalized, science-backed suggestions based on your lifestyle and biomarker data. Each tip includes a projected IRScore gain, helping you take clear, goal-driven action.

Target Market

NexFlow targets health-conscious adults aged 25–55 who are at risk of developing type 2 diabetes. These individuals actively use wearable technology and digital health apps to monitor and improve their health.

Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)

In the U.S., an estimated 98 million adults have prediabetes, which is more than 1 in 3 adults. A large percent of these individuals are unaware they have prediabetes.

Total Addressable Market (TAM)

An estimated 541 million adults have impaired glucose tolerance (IGT). The number is projected to rise to 730 million by 2045.
