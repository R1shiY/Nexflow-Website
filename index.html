Our Solution

NexFlow offers real-time insulin resistance monitoring through non-invasive methods, making early intervention accessible and affordable.

IRScore

A real-time, AI-generated score from 0 to 100 that quantifies your risk of insulin resistance. Simply input lifestyle and biomarker data and receive an easy-to-understand score that updates as new data is logged.

Trajectory Panel

Visualize how your IRScore evolves over time, helping you see the effects of specific habits. Track daily behaviors and biomarker updates to identify patterns, measure progress, and course-correct in real-time.

Recommendation System

Receive personalized, science-backed suggestions based on your lifestyle and biomarker data. Each tip includes a projected IRScore gain, helping you take clear, goal-driven action.

How It Works

Input Your Data

Log your lifestyle factors (sleep, diet, activity) and biomarker data (glucose, insulin levels) through our simple interface. Connect with your existing health apps and wearables for seamless data integration.

Get Your IRScore

Our AI algorithm processes your data to generate a personalized IRScore between 0-100, indicating your current insulin resistance risk level. The score updates in real-time as new data is logged.

Track Progress & Receive Recommendations

Monitor your IRScore trajectory over time and receive personalized recommendations to improve your metabolic health. Each suggestion includes a projected impact on your IRScore.

Competitive Advantage

Unlike competitors that rely on expensive and invasive methods for diabetes management such as CGMs, NexFlow provides real-time insulin resistance estimates using non-invasive lifestyle and biomarker data.
